## Overview

This compilation covers the major defence-related news and updates from December 2025, providing a comprehensive overview for defence aspirants and enthusiasts.

## Exam Notifications

### NDA (I) 2026
The National Defence Academy (I) 2026 examination has been scheduled for **21 April 2026**. The application process is now open, with the last date to apply being **9 January 2026**. This is a crucial opportunity for students who have completed or are appearing for their 12th standard examinations.

### CDS (I) 2026
The Combined Defence Services (I) 2026 notification has been officially released. Candidates interested in joining the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), Air Force Academy (AFA), or Officers Training Academy (OTA) should apply before **9 January 2026**.

### AFCAT 01/2026 Results
The Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT) 01/2026 results have been declared. Successful candidates can check the merit list on the official AFCAT website. The next step for qualified candidates is the SSB interview process.

## SSB Updates

### IMA/OTA SSB Dates
Service Selection Board (SSB) dates for Indian Military Academy (IMA) and Officers Training Academy (OTA) have been announced for CDS (II) 2025 candidates. The SSB interviews will be conducted at various selection centers across India.

### SSB Interview Preparation Tips
- Focus on improving your communication skills
- Practice group discussion and group planning exercises
- Work on your physical fitness
- Stay updated with current affairs
- Develop leadership qualities

## Career Opportunities

### NCC Special Entry Scheme 2026
The NCC Special Entry Scheme 2026 notification has been released. This is an excellent opportunity for eligible NCC cadets to join the Indian Army as officers.

### Indian Navy B.Tech Entry 2026
Applications for Indian Navy B.Tech Entry 2026 are now open. This entry scheme is specifically designed for engineering graduates and students.
